# Hex

The classic connection game (Piet Hein / John Nash), on a rhombus of hexagons.

## Objective
Link your **two opposite edges** with an unbroken chain of your stones.

## Board & setup
An N×N rhombus of hexagonal cells (the **size** option; 11 per side is traditional), empty to start. Red owns the top and bottom edges, Blue the left and right edges. Red moves first.

## Play
On your turn, place one stone of your colour on any empty cell. Stones are never moved or captured.

## Winning & draws
The first player to connect their two edges with a chain of adjacent stones **wins**. By a famous theorem, once the board fills exactly one player has connected — so **draws are impossible**.

## In this implementation
- The board **size** is selectable.
