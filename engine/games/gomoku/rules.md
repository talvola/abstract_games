# Gomoku (Five in a Row)

A two-player game on a square grid (**13×13**, **15×15**, or **19×19**, chosen
with the board-size option). Player 0 is **Black** and moves first; player 1 is
**White**.

## How to play

- On your turn, place one stone on any **empty** cell.
- Stones never move or get captured.

## Winning

The first player to form an unbroken line of **five or more** of their own stones
wins. The line may run:

- **horizontally**,
- **vertically**, or
- **diagonally** (either direction).

If the board fills with no such line, the game is a **draw**.

## Variant

This is the **freestyle** variant: the first player has no opening restrictions,
and an **overline** (six or more in a row) also wins. Freestyle favours the first
player with perfect play; the larger boards make for longer, more open games.
