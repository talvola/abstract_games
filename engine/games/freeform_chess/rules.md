# Freeform Board (8×8)

This is an **unenforced** board — an honor-system sandbox. The platform checks
**no rules**: there is no legal-move highlighting, no turn legality, no check or
checkmate detection, and no winner is decided for you.

## How to play

- The board starts in the standard chess array on an 8×8 grid.
- On your turn, **drag any piece to any square** (or click the piece, then the
  destination). Whatever is on the destination is captured. Moves are *not*
  checked for legality — you and your opponent agree on the rules you are
  playing and police them yourselves.
- Turns simply alternate. Use **Pass** if you want to hand the turn over without
  moving.

## Ending a game

Because nothing is enforced, the result is set by agreement:

- **Resign** — you concede; your opponent wins.
- **Offer draw** — proposes a draw and passes the turn; your opponent may
  **Accept** (the game is a draw) or **Decline** (play continues). Making any
  ordinary move also declines a pending offer.

## What it's for

Playing any 8×8 chess variant by hand, setting up and exploring positions, or
casual games where you'd rather not have a referee. There is **no computer
opponent** for freeform boards.
