# Tic-Tac-Toe

The classic 3×3 alignment game — the platform's minimal reference game.

## Objective
Be the first to place three of your marks in a row.

## Play
- Players alternate. Player 1 (X) moves first, Player 2 (O) second.
- On your turn, place your mark on any empty cell.

## Winning & draws
- The first player to get **three in a row** — horizontally, vertically, or diagonally — wins.
- If all nine cells fill with no three-in-a-row, the game is a **draw**.
